## Frameworks Covered

- **ISO/IEC 27001** — 10 control domains
- **NIS2 Directive** — Article 21 (10 controls)
- **Cyber Essentials** — UK NCSC (5 controls)
- **NIST CSF** — Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover
- **CIS Benchmarks** — Level 1 and Level 2
- **GDPR** — Data protection controls
- **PCI-DSS** — Payment card security
- **SOC 2** — Trust service criteria

## Auto-Generation

Reports are generated by `compliance_report.py` and published to
`/reports/` on the live platform. Each report includes:

- Overall compliance score
- Control-by-control assessment
- Evidence references (SIEM events, scan results)
- Threat intelligence summary
- Priority recommendations
- Report ID for audit trail
